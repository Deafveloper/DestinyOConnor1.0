ABOUT ME

I am deaf, which has given me a deep appreciation for the power of technology and its ability to connect people across barriers. I have always been fascinated by software development and started teaching myself to code several years ago. I have since been able to develop my skills and am now focused on becoming a QA tester. In addition to my software development pursuits, I also have four years of experience as a webmaster. This experience has given me a strong understanding of web development, which I can leverage in my future endeavors. As someone who has experienced the power of technology firsthand, I am committed to using my skills to create inclusive and accessible software. I am excited to continue learning and growing as a developer and can't wait to see where my journey takes me.
